# HO5 — Interactive Artifact Tool

> Hands-on portfolio project · **Week 3** · **Solo** · module M9. Part of the **ForgeMind AI — AI Productivity Essentials** course.

## Goal

**Done when:** A shareable interactive tool built as a Claude Artifact

## What to ship

The Artifact source + README on what it does + a live/hosted link or screenshot.

## Pick a problem statement

Choose **one** of these real use-cases — or bring your own (get it approved first):

1. Prospects keep asking your sales team 'what will this actually save me?' and the answer is a guess. Build an interactive Claude Artifact where a buyer enters a few numbers (team size, hours, current cost) and instantly sees the ROI of your product. Success: a shareable link where changing the inputs updates the result live.

2. Customers on your site cannot tell which of your plans or options fits them. Build a no-code decision quiz as a Claude Artifact that asks a handful of questions and recommends one option with a short reason. Success: a shareable link where two different sets of answers lead to two clearly different, sensible recommendations.

3. A concept in your field confuses every newcomer and your written explainer falls flat. Build an interactive Artifact that explains it with toggles or scenarios the reader can flip between to see how the outcome changes. Success: a shareable link where switching a toggle visibly changes the explanation and makes the idea click.

4. Your audience wants to know 'how ready am I?' for something you teach or sell. Build a self-assessment scorecard as a Claude Artifact that scores their answers and returns a result plus tailored advice. Success: a shareable link where a low score and a high score each return clearly different, useful guidance.

5. People weighing a loan or savings goal cannot picture how the numbers play out over time. Build an interactive calculator as a Claude Artifact where they enter amount, rate and term and see the result with a live chart that redraws. Success: a shareable link where changing any input instantly updates both the figure and the chart.
