# Sample 01 — Competitor Intelligence Knowledge Base (copy-and-use template)

> A ready-to-adapt template for building a NotebookLM knowledge base over your competitors' sites, launch posts, reviews and docs — so you can ask *"what are their three biggest weaknesses?"* and get answers that **cite the exact source**.

**This is a template.** It comes with a worked example already filled in, plus clearly-marked blanks (`<< … >>`) for your own competitors. Clone it, swap in your real sources, and you have your gap-analysis knowledge base.

## The problem statement

> *"As a marketer you can't keep up with what competitors are shipping. Build a NotebookLM knowledge base over their sites, launch posts, reviews and docs, then ask 'what are their three biggest weaknesses?' and get answers that cite the exact source. Success: a notebook + a cited gap-analysis brief + an Audio Overview, documented in your repo."*

## What you'll build

A **research knowledge base** in Google NotebookLM. You add your competitors' real sources, and NotebookLM answers your questions with a **citation next to every claim** — real retrieval + generation (RAG) over *your* material, not the model guessing. That's the honest step up from HO4 (which was pasting text into a prompt, no retrieval).

## Use it with your Claude.ai subscription

No API key, no code. NotebookLM is free with a Google account.

1. Go to **[notebooklm.google.com](https://notebooklm.google.com)** and sign in. Click **Create new notebook**.
2. Click **Add source**. Add your competitors' material — paste a **website URL**, upload a **PDF**, paste **copied text**, or add a **YouTube link**. Use the table in [`sources.md`](./sources.md) as your fill-in list (6–12 sources across 2–3 competitors).
3. In the chat, paste the ready-made question from [`qa.md`](./qa.md): *"What are the three biggest weaknesses customers complain about for each competitor? Cite the source for each."*
4. Every answer has **numbered citations** — click one to jump to the exact sentence. Paste your real answers into `qa.md`.
5. In **Studio**, click **Briefing doc**; copy it into [`briefing.md`](./briefing.md) and sharpen it.
6. In **Studio**, click **Audio Overview → Generate**, then **Share** to get a link. Paste it below.
7. Click **Share** on the notebook to get a viewable link; paste it below.

**No API key needed.**

## The three template files
- **`sources.md`** — a source-list template: one worked example row set + blank rows to fill.
- **`qa.md`** — a cited-Q&A template: one fully-worked example Q&A + four blank slots and ready-to-ask questions.
- **`briefing.md`** — a one-page gap-analysis brief template with example content to overwrite.

## Make it your own
- Replace the example competitors with your real ones. Add a **review** source (G2/Reddit/Capterra) per competitor — that's where honest weaknesses live.
- Re-run the questions monthly; add each new launch post and ask *"what changed since last quarter?"*
