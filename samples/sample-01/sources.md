## Recipe (why these source types)
- **One marketing page per competitor** — how they *position* themselves (their claims).
- **One launch/blog post** — what they *just shipped*.
- **One review source per competitor** — where the honest weaknesses surface.
- **One pricing/docs page** — hard limits they don't advertise.

Mixing their claims with customers' reality is what lets NotebookLM answer *"where's the gap between promise and experience?"* with citations on both sides.

## Tips
- If a review page needs a login, copy the visible reviews into a **"Paste text"** source.
- Keep each source focused; split very long PDFs so retrieval stays sharp.
