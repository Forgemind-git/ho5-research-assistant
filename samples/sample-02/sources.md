## Recipe (start with the highest-value docs)
- **Access & security first** — the questions people are most nervous to ask twice.
- **Runbooks** — so they can self-serve their first deploy / on-call shift.
- **Standards & directory** — "how we work" and "who to ask".
- **HR policy** — PTO/leave questions that don't belong in a Slack DM.

## Tips
- Export Confluence/Notion pages to **PDF** or **paste** them as text — NotebookLM reads both.
- One topic per source so citations land on the right page.
