## How the sources were chosen
- **Access & security first** (`S2`, `S8`) — the questions new joiners are most nervous to ask twice.
- **Runbooks** (`S3`, `S4`) — so they can self-serve during their first deploy or on-call shift.
- **Standards & directory** (`S6`, `S7`) — "how do we work" and "who do I ask".
- **HR policy** (`S5`) — PTO/leave questions that don't belong in a Slack DM.

## Tips for your own version
- Export Confluence/Notion pages to **PDF** or copy them as **pasted text** — NotebookLM reads both.
- Keep one topic per source so citations land on the right page.
- Re-upload a doc when it changes; NotebookLM answers from the version you gave it.
