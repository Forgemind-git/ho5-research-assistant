## Recipe (cover each option evenly)
- **Pricing PDF per option** — the number the decision hinges on, plus what's excluded.
- **Feature/docs page per option** — capabilities and, crucially, **data export / lock-in**.
- **One third-party review per option** — the reality behind the sales pitch.
- **One neutral comparison** — so NotebookLM can balance vendor claims.

## Tips
- For a **health plan**: upload each plan's **Summary of Benefits PDF** — ask "which covers X and what's the deductible?".
- Include the **contract/terms PDF** if you can — cancellation and lock-in clauses are where you most want a citation.
