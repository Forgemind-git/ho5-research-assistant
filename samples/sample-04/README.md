# HO5 Sample 4 — Self-Assessment

## Your task

Your audience wants to know "how ready am I?" Build a self-assessment scorecard Artifact with 8–10 yes/no or rated questions, an auto-scored result, and personalised tips.

## What to do

1. Open index.html in your browser — you should see the skeleton layout
2. Read the TODO comments in the HTML and JavaScript
3. Fill in: your 8–10 questions, the scoring bands, and the personalised tips per weak area
4. Test it by answering all questions with different combinations
5. Push to GitHub and enable Pages for a shareable link

## Inputs your tool needs

- 8–10 questions (yes/no or 1–5 rating)

## Outputs your tool should show

- Total score
- Band label (e.g. Beginner / Practitioner / Expert)
- Personalised tip per weak area
