## Recipe
- **Exam guide/syllabus first** — so the study guide and FAQ are targeted to what's tested.
- **Core readings** — one per exam domain, so retrieval covers the whole syllabus.
- **Your own notes as pasted text** — NotebookLM will cite them too and reconcile them against the official readings.

## Tips
- Smaller, focused sources = sharper citations. Split a 200-page textbook into the chapters you actually need.
- Upload the **official exam guide** — it makes the generated study guide far more useful.
