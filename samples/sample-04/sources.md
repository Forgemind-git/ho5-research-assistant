## How the sources were chosen
- **Exam guide first** (`S1`) — so the notebook knows the domain weightings and can prioritise.
- **Core whitepapers** (`S2`, `S6`) — the dense material the exam is really testing.
- **Service overviews** (`S3`, `S4`, `S5`, `S8`) — one per exam domain, so retrieval covers the whole syllabus.
- **Your own notes** (`S7`) — pasted text counts as a source; NotebookLM will cite your notes too.

## Tips for your own version
- Upload the **official exam guide** — it makes the study guide and FAQ far more targeted.
- Split a giant whitepaper into the 2–3 chapters you actually need; smaller sources = sharper citations.
- Add your **own notes as pasted text** so the notebook can reconcile them against the official readings.
