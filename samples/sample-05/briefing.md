## TL;DR for the team
Three shifts this quarter: **organic search CTR is falling** (AI Overviews), **paid social got more expensive**, and **AI tooling adoption surged**. Conversion rates are flat — so with acquisition costs rising, our biggest lever is **converting the traffic we already get**.

## What we should do
1. **Rebalance SEO** toward branded and bottom-funnel content; expect less free informational traffic `[S4]`.
2. **Fix creative efficiency on paid social** before adding budget — CPMs are up, so creative is the ROI lever `[S2]`.
3. **Adopt AI for content throughput** so we don't fall behind on output `[S3]`.
4. **Prioritise CRO this quarter** — flat conversion + rising CAC means converting existing traffic beats buying more `[S2][S7]`.
5. **Keep segmenting email** — it's protecting engagement despite noisier opens `[S1]`.
