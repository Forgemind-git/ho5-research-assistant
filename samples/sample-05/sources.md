## Recipe
- **One report per major area** of your field, so the notebook can answer "what changed" across the board.
- **A cross-cutting trend deck** — captures shifts that touch everything.
- **At least one analyst/opinion source** — so the notebook can answer "why does it matter", not just "what's the number".
- **A video briefing** is fine — NotebookLM ingests YouTube.

## Tips
- Add reports from **this quarter** and remove last quarter's so "what changed" stays honest.
- If a 50-page report retrieves vaguely, split its executive summary out as its own source.
