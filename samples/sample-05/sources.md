## How the sources were chosen
- **One report per major channel** (email `S1`, paid social `S2`, search `S4`, content `S5`, CRO `S7`) — so the notebook can answer "what changed" across the whole funnel.
- **A cross-cutting trend deck** (`S3`) — captures tooling/AI shifts that touch every channel.
- **A video briefing** (`S6`) — NotebookLM ingests YouTube; analyst webinars often break news before the PDF lands.

## Tips for your own version
- Add reports from **this quarter** and remove last quarter's so "what changed" stays honest.
- Long reports (50+ pages) are fine, but if retrieval feels vague, split out the executive-summary section as its own source.
- Include at least one **analyst/opinion** source alongside raw benchmarks so the notebook can answer "why does it matter", not just "what's the number".
